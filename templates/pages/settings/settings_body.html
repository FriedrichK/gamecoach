If you choose to become a mentor, your profile will be shown in the mentor search results. You will also begin to receive coaching requests from students. As a mentor, you can still ask other mentors to coach you. If you get tired of mentoring, you can always switch to being a student.

Become a Student
If you choose to become a student, your profile will no longer be shown in the mentor search results. You will not receive coaching requests from students. If you change your mind you can become a mentor at any time.
